- Keep anchor text varied and natural
- Do not stuff identical anchors across every page
- Prioritise contextual fit over volume
- Avoid linking from unrelated pages just to create more backlinks

## Usage Notes

### When to use each asset

- Use the guest post pitches when approaching editors, education publications, and community managers.
- Use the HARO-style responses when replying quickly to journalist requests or expert quote roundups.
- Use the backlink plan when updating sister domains, founder pages, and older educational content.
- Use the testimonial template once a beta user has had enough product exposure to give concrete feedback.

### How to personalise lightly

- Change the opening line to reference one recent article, theme, or editorial priority.
- Swap one outline bullet so it matches the publication’s audience more closely.
- Keep the founder bio consistent unless a shorter version is explicitly needed.
- Keep the Zaza Draft mention soft unless the editor requests a more product-focused angle.

### How to keep claims factual and low-risk

- Do not cite numbers unless they are already published and verified.
- Do not suggest endorsement from a publication, directory, or community.
- Do not describe Zaza Draft as autonomous or as a replacement for teacher judgement.
- Keep all product framing tied to parent communication, report writing, school writing, tone, and teacher control.
