# Programmatic Quality Checklist

Use this checklist before a page family is shipped or expanded.

## Value test

- Does the page solve a specific teacher wording problem?
- Is the page still useful even if the CTA is removed?
- Are the examples realistically editable by a tired teacher at the end of the day?
- Would the page still make sense to a UK teacher, middle leader, or SLT reviewer?

## Content quality

- Minimum useful word count: `900`
- Target range: `1,050 to 1,500`
- At least `4` substantial body sections
- At least `4` examples, ideally `5` or `6`
- At least `6` FAQs
- At least `5` related internal links plus the hub

## Tone check

- Sounds calm, not salesy
- Sounds teacher-first, not broad-platform
- Uses UK English
- Avoids hype language
- Reassures without overclaiming
- Mentions teacher control clearly

## Safety check

- No invented pupil facts
- No invented parent motives
- No implication that Zaza Draft sends or decides anything automatically
- No legal-risky competitor claims
- No fake testimonials
- No unsupported GDPR or safeguarding promises

## SEO check

- Keyword in title
- Keyword in H1
- Keyword in first-screen copy
- Keyword in at least one H2
- Keyword in meta description
- Related teacher terms included naturally
- Internal links relevant, not padded

## Conversion check

- CTA feels calm and low-pressure
- Product is framed as a co-writer
- The draft teaser shows a path into Zaza Draft
- Trust block covers teacher-built, GDPR-aware, no hallucinations, and teacher approval

## Expansion rule

Do not scale a route family just because the matrix allows it.

Scale only when:

- impressions are arriving
- ranking behaviour is stable
- page quality review passes
- trial starts or waitlist signups justify the cluster
