# Zaza Draft Short-Form Video Content System

Purpose: help Zaza Draft publish calm, useful short-form videos for TikTok, YouTube Shorts, Instagram Reels, and X that speak directly to exhausted UK teachers and turn real teacher pain into trust, clicks, and trial sign-ups.

Audience: classroom teachers, tutors, heads of year, pastoral leads, SEN staff, form tutors, and report-weary school staff who are dealing with difficult wording after hours.

Voice: calm colleague. Specific, empathetic, useful. Never hype. Never flashy. Never "AI fixes everything". Zaza Draft is the co-writer that helps teachers get the wording right while they stay fully in control.

Core rule: every video should feel helpful even if the viewer never clicks. The product mention is there to reduce stress, not to hard-sell.

## Content Principles

- Start with a teacher moment that feels painfully real.
- Name the emotional load quickly.
- Give one practical structure, script, or wording idea.
- Mention Zaza Draft softly as the tool behind the calmer draft.
- End with one simple next step.
- Keep visuals steady, minimal, and professional.

## 6. Analytics and Iteration Tips

Track the full path from attention to sign-up:

1. Views
2. Average watch time
3. Completion rate
4. Saves and shares
5. Profile visits
6. Link clicks
7. Landing-page conversion rate
8. Trial starts or waitlist sign-ups

### What to review weekly

- Top 3 hooks by watch rate
- Top 3 captions by click-through rate
- Best-performing pain category
- Best-performing landing page by trial conversion
- Comments that reveal new pain language to reuse

### What to test next

- Hook phrased as a question vs blunt statement
- Talking-head delivery vs screen-led before-and-after
- CTA in spoken audio vs caption only
- `Link in bio` vs `Comment for the guide`
- 20-second clips vs 40-second clips

### Pain themes likely to outperform

1. Angry parent replies
2. Weekend email drain
3. Admin pressure and contact logs
4. Report comment wording dread
5. Behaviour emails that do not escalate

### Simple dashboard recommendation

Track each video in one sheet with:

- Date posted
- Platform
- Video ID
- Topic
- Hook
- Target page
- UTM link
- Views
- Completion rate
- Comments
- Saves
- Link clicks
- Trial starts

### Scale rule

Double down when one pain topic does all three:

- above-average watch time
- above-average link clicks
- above-average trial starts

If a video gets views but not clicks:

- strengthen the CTA
- swap the landing page
- test a clearer keyword in the title

If a video gets clicks but not trials:

- the message-to-page match is off
- the CTA may be too broad
- try sending traffic to the more specific pain-first landing page
