- Meta description: `Explore a topical hub on teacher parent communication, safe AI writing help, report comments, difficult emails, and calmer school communication workflows.`
- H1: `Teacher parent communication hub`

Hero summary:

Teachers do not need louder AI. They need safer help with the writing that carries emotional weight, such as parent complaints, behaviour updates, report comments, documentation for SLT, and follow-up after parents’ evening. This hub brings together Zaza Draft guides on safe AI writing help for teachers, including parent emails, report comments, SEN wording, and ways to reduce workload without sounding generic. Zaza Draft is a teacher-first co-writer. Teachers stay in control, review every line, and use AI to reduce stress rather than create new risk.
