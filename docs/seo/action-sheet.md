## Action Labels

- `KEEP`: retain current indexation and sitemap handling
- `IMPROVE`: keep the section, but strengthen linking, metadata, sitemap rules, or freshness
- `NOINDEX`: keep crawl access where useful, but remove the pages from the index
- `CANONICALISE`: consolidate parameter or duplicate intent to a stronger canonical URL
- `REMOVE FROM SITEMAP`: keep the URL accessible if needed, but stop advertising it for discovery
- `CONSOLIDATE`: merge overlapping families or reduce competing route shapes
- `PRUNE`: remove weak or overproduced pages from active SEO inventory
- `REVIEW MANUALLY`: assess with performance, uniqueness, and business-value checks before deciding
