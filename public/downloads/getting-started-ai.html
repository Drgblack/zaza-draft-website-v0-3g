Getting Started with AI

What is AI?

Artificial Intelligence (AI) refers to computer systems that can perform tasks that typically require human intelligence-like understanding language, recognizing patterns, and solving problems.

Why Use AI in the Classroom?

  • Save Time: Automate lesson planning, grading feedback, and administrative tasks
  • Personalize Learning: Generate differentiated materials for different student levels
  • Provide Instant Feedback: Students receive immediate, detailed feedback on their work
  • Enhance Creativity: Brainstorm lesson ideas and create engaging activities
  • Support Diverse Learners: Generate materials for ELL students, IEPs, and advanced learners

Common Concerns Addressed

Will AI replace teachers? No. AI is a tool to enhance your capabilities, not replace them.

Student privacy? Always use FERPA-ready tools and never input personally identifiable information.

Not tech-savvy enough? If you can type a question, you can use AI. No coding required.
